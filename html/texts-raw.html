The Book of Etiquette and Ceremonial 《儀禮》 Pre-Han Prose Also known as the Yili, an early classic Chinese Text describing details of ceremonies
Rites of Zhou 《周禮》 Pre-Han Prose Also known as the Zhouli, an early classic Chinese Text describing the court structure nd official positions
Book of Changes 《易經》 Pre-Han Bilingual prose Also known as the I Ching or 周易 Zhouyi, an early classic Chinese text on divination with English translation by James Legge

Mencius 《孟子》 c. 372-289 BCE Bilingual prose Also known as Mengzi, with English translation by James Legge

Guanzi 《管子》 Pre-Qin Prose Traditionally attributed to statesman 管仲 Zhan Zhong
Huainanzi 《淮南子》 Before 139 BCE Prose An early collection of debates in the court of 劉安 Liu An
Xunzi 《荀子》 - c. 237 BCE Prose An early master with text of the same name
Han Feizi 《韓非子》 c. 280-233 BCE Prose An early master with text of the same name

Standard Histories (正史)      
Records of the Grand Historian 《史記》 109-91 BCE Prose The first of the standard Chinese histories by 司馬遷 Sima Qian with a partial English translation
Book of Han 《漢書》 111 Prose The second of the standard Chinese histories by 班固 Ban Gu with others, covering the 西漢 Western [Former] Han dynasty 206 BCE - 25 CE
Book of Later Han 《後漢書》 5th century Prose The third of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 範曄 Fan Ye, covering the 東漢 Eastern [Later] Han dynasty 25-220 CE
Records of the Three Kingdoms 《三國志》 C. 289 Prose The fourth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 陳壽 Chen Shou and annotations by 裴松之 Pei Songzhi, covering the end of the Eastern Han dynasty and the Three Kingdoms (220–280)
Book of Jin 《晉書》 C. 648 Prose The fifth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 房玄齡 Fang Xuanling, covering the Jin dynasty (265-420)
Book of Song 《宋書》 C. 493 Prose The sixth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 沈約 Shen Yue, covering the Liu Song dynasty (420–479)
Book of Southern Qi 《南齊書》 537 Prose The seventh of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 蕭子顯 Xiao Zixian, covering the Southern Qi dynasty (479-502)
Book of Liang 《梁書》 636 Prose The eigth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 姚思廉 Yao Silian, covering the Liang dynasty (502–557)
Book of Chen 《陳書》 636 Prose The ninth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 姚思廉 Yao Silian, covering the Chen dynasty (557-58)
Book of Wei 《魏書》 554 Prose The tenth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 魏收 Wei Shou, covering the Northern Wei and Eastern Wei dynasties from 386 to 550
Book of Northern Qi 《北齊書》 554 Prose The eleventh of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 李百藥 Li Baiyao, covering the Northern Qi dynasty (550-577)
Book of Zhou 《周書》 636 Prose The twelfth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 令狐德棻 Linghu Defen, covering the Northern Zhou dynasty (557-581)
Book of Sui 《隋書》 636 Prose The thirteenth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 魏徵 Wei Zheng, 令狐德棻 Linghu Defen, and 長孫無忌 Zhangsun Wuji covering the Sui dynasty (581-618)
History of the Southern Dynasties 《南史》 659 Prose The fourteenth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 李延壽 Li Yanshou, covering the Liu Song, Southern Qi, Liang, and Chen dynasties
History of the Northern Dynasties 《北史》 659 Prose The fifteenth of the standard Chinese histories, compiled by 李延壽 Li Yanshou, covering the Northern Wei, Western Wei, Eastern Wei, Northern Zhou, Northern Qi, and Sui dynasties
