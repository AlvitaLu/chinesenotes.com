基本笔画（颜体） Fundamental Strokes (Yanti)

There are twenty two fundamental strokes and each is described below for the Yan style (颜体).

左上点 Upper Left Dot

      <div class="picture">
        <a href='illustrations_use.php?mediumResolution=upper_left_dot.png'><img class='use' 
           src='images/upper_left_dot.png' alt='Upper Left Dot' 
           title='Upper Left Dot'/></a>
        <div>
         左上点
         Upper Left Dot
        </div>
      </div>

Start the stroke with a hidden tip
Turn the brush moving left
Turn the brush moving down and right
Move back in a transition
Move the brush out and up off the paper
This stroke is used in the characters 为, 精, and 禜,.

上点 Upper Dot

        <a href='illustrations_use.php?mediumResolution=upper_dot.png'><img class='use' 
           src='images/upper_dot.png' alt='Upper Dot' 
           title='Upper Dot'/></a>
        <div>
         上点 Upper Dot
        </div>
      </div>

Start the stroke with a hidden tip
Move the tip to the right
Turn the brush moving down and left
Move back and stop
An upper dot (上点 or 斜点) is used in the characters 主, 文, 之, 夜, 空, and 度.

左厥 Left Combined Horizontal and Vertical Hook Stroke

      <div class="picture">
        <a href='illustrations_use.php?mediumResolution=zuo_jue.png'><img class='use' 
           src='images/zuo_jue.png' alt='Left Combined Horizontal and Vertical Hook Stroke'

        </div>

Start the stroke with a hidden tip

Reduce pressure again and then move right
