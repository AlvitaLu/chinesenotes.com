美国会通过临时拨款法案避免联邦政府关门

The United States Congress passed a temporary bill for allocation of funds to avoid the federal government shutting down

美国国会参议院９日深夜通过一项为期４个多月的临时拨款法案，以避免联邦政府关门。

US Congress and Senate passed a bill late at night on the 9th for a four month temporary allocation of funds to avoid a federal government shutdown.
