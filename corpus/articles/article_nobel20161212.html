２０１６年诺贝尔奖颁奖仪式在斯德哥尔摩举行

The 2016 Nobel Prize ceremony is held in Stockholm

２０１６年诺贝尔物理学奖、化学奖、生理学或医学奖、文学奖和经济学奖颁奖仪式１０日在瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩举行，文学奖得主鲍勃·迪伦缺席颁奖仪式。

The 2016 Award Ceremony for Nobel Prizes in Physics, Chemistry, Physiology or Medicine, Literature, and Economics were held on the 10th in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden. The prize winner for literature, Bob Dylan, was absent from the ceremony.
