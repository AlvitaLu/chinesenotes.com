航天技术助力智能制造转型升级

Aerospace Technology uses Intelligent Manufacturing to Transform and Upgrade

北京航天智造科技有限公司副总经理侯宝存介绍：这套虚拟工厂平台，通过虚拟现实技术、仿真技术、物联网技术构建与实际工厂环境、生产能力和生产过程完全对应的虚拟生产环境，实现“数字双胞胎”意义的虚拟工厂。

Beijing Aerospace Intelligent Technology Company Limited Assistant General Manager Hou Baocun explained: this virtual factory platform uses virtual reality technology, simulation technology, and Internet of Things technology to build a real factory environment with production capability and complete production processes corresponding to the virtual production environment. This realizes the 'digital twins' vision of the virtual factory.
